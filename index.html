Lecture Lyft

"Hypothesis: Real-time, AI-driven lecture transcription and context-aware analysis significantly improves student learning retention and note-taking efficiency compared to traditional methods."

Problem Statement

Students struggle to keep pace with fast-moving lectures, leading to incomplete notes and missed critical concepts. Traditional recording methods lack searchability and context, making review inefficient. There is a need for a system that not only captures but *understands* and *organizes* lecture content in real-time.

Research Gap / Innovation

Lecture Lyft integrates Groq's ultra-low latency inference with a local transcription engine to provide instant summaries. Crucially, we implemented a Context Injection Mechanism where topic-specific keywords are dynamically generated and fed into Whisper to bias recognition accuracy.

Hallucination Handling

Leak Prevention: Since injecting prompts can cause models to "hallucinate" or repeat the prompt, we implemented a regex-based Leak Filter that strips out echoed prompt text from the final output.
Silence Filtering: VAD (Voice Activity Detection) ensures only valid speech segments are processed.
